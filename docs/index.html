Digital inpatient list · NHS‑style

A smarter way to find and follow inpatients.

findpatient is a lightweight, browser‑based inpatient list designed to feel familiar to NHS teams while reducing friction, duplication and missed information.

  • Built for: ward teams, on‑call doctors, and multidisciplinary workflows.
  • Focus: clarity, safety, and rapid situational awareness at the bedside.
  • Hosting: secure, GitHub Pages–backed prototype environment.

Clean lists. Clear priorities. Less cognitive load.
